BayT: Baybayin Translator

Baybayin translators are already available on the web and on smartphones but all of them lack the capability to translate Baybayin texts through images. There are some keyboards available on smartphones that support Baybayin but since the the writing system is a bit complicated, the application will help the users translate the text to English without typing them.

Brand Trends Analyzer using Big Data

With the popularization of social media, Twitter has become a self-reporting platform for various topics. With the analyzation of keywords and hashtags, we can produce faster feedback on various products of a company thus making it easier to determine needs for changes and address issues. With Natural Language Processing, Big Data can be used to formulate and conclude the most common problems, and identify and categorize opinions using sentiment analysis regarding the product. Feedback will be easily delivered to the company owning the product.

Canvas Monitoring and Evaluator System

Canvas has become the recent learning management platform of most schools and universities today including FEU- Institute of Technology.The authors of this proposal's purpose is to have a grade and performance analyzer and evaluator to easily summarize the performance of the students. It will also help students who have scatter focus on where to give attention on the topics discussed per course.

Averagely, FEU-TECH students have five to seven subjects per trimester and the proponents of this proposals sees the need to help them improve their academic standing despite the tight schedule.
